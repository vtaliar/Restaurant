Delectable Entrees
The taste of a great meal stays with us, and its flavors and textures stay with us long after we walk away from the table, and making your meal memorial is why we only use choice, fresh quality cuts of meat, cooked in their own juices, seasoned with herbs and spices at the height of their flavor. Our delicious entrees are sure to please the heartiest and most discriminating of appetites. We create the ultimate eating experience just for you with a classic menu of tempting treats.
Prime Rib on Fridays
The ultimate dinner fit for a king, our juicy tender prime cut beef is rubbed and seasoned with the finest fresh herbs and spices, crisped and then slow roasted to its peak of flavor.
Turkey Breast
Classic and light, our tender young turkey breast is roasted with delicate herbs and spices, to a moist and tender perfection.
Sausage (Hand Made Beef)
A culinary treat, our own hand made sausage is a taste sensation, stuffed with tender morsels of beef and seasoned to perfection, and then gently pan fried to sizzling perfection.
Brisket
Subtle and tasty, this irresistible, tender and flavorful cut of beef is rubbed with just the right spices and herbs, then braised and slowly simmered in its own juices.
Beef Short Ribs

Pork Ribs
Truly a rib lovers delight, these succulent roasted pork ribs, seasoned to perfection with herbs and spices are falling off the bone ideal. Tender and crisp with a hearty flavor.
Pork Chop 1 1/2" thick
Succulent and satisfying, our extra thick pork chops are grilled to perfection with fresh herbs and spices to enhance their natural tenderness and flavor.
